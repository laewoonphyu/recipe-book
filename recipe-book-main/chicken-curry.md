#Chicken Curry

##Ingredients
- 6 Chicken thighs
- One onion
- Three colves of gralic
- Hatch Red Medium pepper (optional) (can add accordingly to your spice tolerance)
- Fish sauce
- Tumeric powder
- Paprika

##Cooking Steps
1. Mix the chicken thighs with salt, daishi, and tumeric powder and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
2. Chop the onions, pepper, and garlic nicely. 
3. Put oil in a flat pan and saute the onions, pepper, and garlic until golden brown.
4. Sear the chicken thighs in a hot pan until golden.
5. Put around 300ml of water and simmer until the chicek is fully cooked. 
6. Do taste testing with the curry and put in seasoning if needed.
